Description:

The Office of Teaching, Learning, & Technology will offer instructional technology training sessions on the date(s) and time(s) listed below. All training sessions below will be held in the University Capitol Centre Training Room (located on the second floor of the UCC building). To learn more about available instructional technology and trainings, visit the Office of Teaching, Learning & Technology website. ICON Overview - Click the date below to register: -Monday, August 10, 9:30 - 11:30 am (REGISTRATION FULL) -Wednesday, August 12, 9:30 - 11:30 am -Monday, August 17, 1:30 - 3:30 pm (REGISTRATION FULL) Receive an overview of the new version of ICON and learn how to use ICON’s Content tool to make course materials and related links available to your students in a timely, easily accessible manner. Practice uploading files and creating Content Modules (folders) to organize student links to these files. Learn how to link to external websites or related ICON tools—like a Quiz or Discussion. Learn how to restrict content availability to certain times or students and how to view your Content as a student would. If you cannot make it to a training or you do not see the topic you need covered, we also offer individual appointments. Additionally, we offer training on a variety of instructional tools such as ICON, UICapture/Panopto, advanced clickers, ShareStream, TurnItIn, the Wiki, and more. With advanced planning, we can come to your location and do a group training for your department, as well. Please contact the ITS Help Desk for more information.
